Best Chocolate Cake

The Best Chocolate Cake Recipe – quick, easy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (240 g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (396 g) sugar
  • 3/4 cup (63 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons espresso powder (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water, or freshly brewed strong hot coffee
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk, or buttermilk, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180º C (350º F). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. Stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, salt and expresso powder in large bowl.
  3. Add eggs, milk, vegetable oil and vanilla; beat on medium speed of mixer 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in 1 cup of boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour batter into prepared pans.
  6. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
  7. Cool 10 minutes; remove from pans to wire racks. Cool completely.
  8. Enjoy it!
