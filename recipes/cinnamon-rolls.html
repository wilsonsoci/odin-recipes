Best Cinnamon Rolls

The Best Cinnamon Rolls Recipe – fluffy, easy, and delicious!

Ingredients

    For the Rolls:

  • 2 3/4 cup flour (360g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (50g) white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 stick (50g) melted butter
  • 3/4 cup (180ml) warm milk
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 packet (8g) dried yeast

    • For the Filling:

  • 1/2 stick (50g) butter
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 tablespoon corn starch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

    • For the Cream Cheese Glaze:

  • 1/2 cup (100g) cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup (40g) powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Splash of milk. Start with 2 tbsp and adjust to your liking. For a thinner glaze add more milk. If you want more glaze, double the glaze recipe

Instructions

    For the Filling:

  1. Combine the brown sugar, cinnamon, corn starch and salt.

    2. For the Rolls:

  2. In a large bowl combine flour, sugar and salt together.
  3. Warm the milk and add egg, melted butter and yeast.
  4. Combine the dry ingredients and the wet ingredients.
  5. Knead the dough on a work surface for 10 minutes. If you choose to use a stand mixer knead the dough on high for 3 minutes.
  6. Transfer the dough to a greased bowl, cover and allow the dough to rise for 2 hours.
  7. Roll out the dough onto a floured surface into a rectangle. Spread the soft butter over the dough and sprinkle over the cinnamon-sugar mixture. Tightly roll the dough into a jelly roll form and cut out the cinnamon rolls. Place them on a greased baking sheet. Cover with plastic wrap and let them rise again for 30 minutes.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 °C (350 °F) for 20 - 22 minutes.

    9. For the Cream Cheese Glaze:

  9. Mix together the cream cheese, powdered sugar, vanilla, milk.
  10. Enjoy it!
