Best Chocolate Chips Cookies

The Best Chocolate Chips Cookies Recipe – quick, easy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 stick (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup + 1 tbsp (75g) brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup + 1 tbsp (75g) white granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups (200g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup (150g) roughly chopped chocolate

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F)
  2. In a large bowl whisk together the flour, salt and baking soda to combine and set aside.
  3. In a separate bowl cut the butter into small pieces and microwave until melted. Do not let the butter become hot. If it does, wait 10 minutes before making the cookies.
  4. To the melted butter add the brown sugar, granulated sugar, and vanilla and mix until combined and emulsified. Whisk in your egg until completely incorporated.
  5. Using a spatula combine the flour mixture with the butter mixture and mix until the dough is well combined and there are no more visible spots of flour.
  6. Stir in chocolate and using a large ice cream scoop, spoon 8 balls of dough onto a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Leave enough space between each cookie as these cookies will spread out in the oven.
  7. Bake it for about 12 to 15 minutes.
  8. Allow the cookies to sit on the cookie sheet for 3 minutes.
  9. Enjoy it!
